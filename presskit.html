About the Studio

OnlyGrapes is an indie game development studio that was never supposed to exist. Founded at 2am in a Discord voice lobby by four chronically online individuals with too much free time and no business plan, it somehow became a real company. Nobody is more surprised than us.

We make games the kind we'd actually want to play. Every project starts with someone saying "okay hear me out" and spirals from there. We run on diabolical ideas, sleep debt, and the shared belief that the games we want don't exist yet — so we build them ourselves.
