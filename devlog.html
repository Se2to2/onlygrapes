Update Sept 10, 2025
okay hear me out — we're actually doing this

OnlyGrapes is officially a thing. We have a name, a logo, a Discord server with toolittle channels, and a shared Google Doc that is mostly just memes (Literally). First post. No idea what we're doing. Let's go.

So here's the situation: four people in a Discord call at 2am decided to start a game studio. No Ideas. Just the unwavering belief that the games we actually want to play don't exist yet and that we should be the ones to build them.

We have a name. We have a logo that Jai made in one sitting while the rest of us watched and contributed nothing useful. We have a Discord server that already has like 7 channels and counting, most of which will never be used. And we have a Google Doc titled "ideas" that is currently 90% memes and 10% one actual idea that might be kind of.. who are we kidding we don't have any ideas.

This is the first devlog. There will be more. We have no idea where this goes.

Design Oct 22, 2025
Procasinating at 3am: a survival guide

Three weeks ago, Jai said he'd handle the character design. We believed him. That was our second mistake.

Week 1: Silence. Understandable. Great art takes time. We respect the process.

Week 2: Still nothing. Slight concern. Maybe he's cooking something insane. We let him cook.

Week 3: The kitchen is on fire. There is no food.

At this point, we're not sure if the character design exists in a higher plane of existence or if it's just an idea Jai visits in his dreams and refuses to bring back with him.

No sketches. No concepts. Not even a badly drawn stick figure to prove life.

So we waited. Longer than we should have. Checking for updates like it was going to magically appear one day. It didn't. But hope is a powerful and dangerous thing, and we held onto it anyway.

Audio Oct 7, 2025
The Dash Heard 'Round the World

We needed a dash sound

An idea was made.

Sound was required.

The concept: a robot.

The feature: it dashes.

The reality: we now need audio for… that.

Week 1: Silence. Understandable. Great audio takes time.

Week 2: Still nothing. The robot moves. It dashes. Our hopes for normal sound fade.

Week 3: Finally, audio arrives. And somehow… its sounds like ass clapping.

No one asked for it. No one expected it. And yet, it exists. Every dash now punctuated by a sound that makes zero sense.

Current Status:

One dashing robot.

One meme-level sound effect.

Team morale: laughing their ass out.
