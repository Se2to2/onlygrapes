About Us

We make games.
Grapes make wine.
Both age well.

OnlyGrapes is a game development company that was never supposed to exist. It started — as all great and terrible things do — in a Discord voice lobby at 2am, where a group of chronically online individuals with too much free time and absolutely no self-control decided that, yes, we should make games. Nobody stopped us. That was the first mistake.

And that was it. No business plan. No investors. No adults in the room. Just pure unhinged and a shared brain cell that spiraled into something that is somehow, against all odds, a real company. We are as surprised as you are.

We run entirely on diabolical ideas, sleep debt, and the deranged belief that the games we'd want to play don't exist yet — so we have to build them ourselves. Every project starts with someone saying "okay hear me out" followed by an idea that makes one person go "bro that's actually kinda fire"
